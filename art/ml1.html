Vectors Revisited

Elements from vector space V are called vectors (abstract algebra)

or

Vector (denoted by x⃗ ) is a quantity having both magnitude and direction

Above are the most common definitions of vector which you may have read from books or internet sources like Wikipedia. In Machine learning vectors are specifically called feature vectors.
